Workplace dispute triage without the corporate fog machine.

The HR Risk Assessment Tool is a three-tier product for turning messy workplace disputes into clearer risk levels, documentation notes, escalation indicators, and next-step guidance. Stripe checkout links can be added once the product tiers are created.

Starter

Single-case review, basic risk level, documentation gaps, and simple next steps.

Professional

Expanded scoring, policy concern flags, response language, and structured summary.

Premium

Deeper narrative, escalation indicators, action checklist, and higher-sensitivity review.
