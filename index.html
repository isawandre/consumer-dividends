Buyer Questions

What HR purchasers need answered before checkout.

This section exists to remove hesitation. HR buyers do not impulse purchase opaque service reports without understanding process, confidentiality, and deliverables.

How fast is turnaround?

Standard delivery is within 24 hours after materials are submitted. Priority monthly clients are handled first in queue.

What do I receive?

A structured written workplace risk report summarizing the incident, key exposure areas, documentation gaps, and recommended next steps.

Is submitted information confidential?

Yes. Complaint narratives, internal notes, screenshots, manager summaries, and attached documents are used only for the purchased review workflow.

Who typically buys ClearCase HR?

Fractional HR consultants, small agencies, operations managers, founders, and internal leads dealing with messy employee issues without dedicated counsel.

Is this legal advice?

No. ClearCase HR is a structured workplace documentation and exposure review product, designed to improve internal handling before outside escalation.

Can agencies white-label reports?

Yes. Agency plan clients can request co-branded or white-label formatted report delivery for recurring consultant use.
