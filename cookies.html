Cookie Policy

Consumer Dividends Holding Corp

Effective date: June 14, 2026

This page explains what cookies and similar technologies we use on consumerdividends.com and inside our products.

1. What cookies are

A cookie is a small text file a website places on your device to remember information across pages or sessions. We also use comparable technologies such as local storage where appropriate.

2. What we use

We use a small number of strictly necessary cookies and equivalents:

  • Session cookies: keep you logged in or keep your in-app state during a single visit. Expire when you close the browser.
  • Security cookies: detect abuse, prevent CSRF attacks, and protect login flows. Set by our infrastructure providers (Cloudflare, Railway).
  • Stripe: when you go through checkout, Stripe sets cookies on its own pages to process the payment and prevent fraud. See Stripe's policy at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Local storage (in app): we use browser local storage to keep your in-app preferences (such as last-used filter selections in the Numidian dashboard). This stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

3. What we do not use

We do not use:

  • Advertising cookies.
  • Cross-site tracking cookies.
  • Third-party analytics that profile individual users (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Segment, etc.).
  • Social media tracking pixels (Facebook Pixel, LinkedIn Insight, etc.).

If we ever add product analytics, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for your consent before setting any non-essential cookies.

4. Your choices

Strictly necessary cookies do not require consent under GDPR, the ePrivacy Directive, or CCPA, but you can still control them through your browser settings. Blocking session cookies will prevent the product from working.

You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser's privacy settings.

5. Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control

We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal as an opt-out of any sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information in any case.

6. Changes

We update this policy when our practices change. Material updates are reflected in the "Effective date" above.
